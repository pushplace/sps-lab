How it works

From one URL to
a shipped product.

The entire integration is four steps. Most of them happen without you.

1

Share the docs URL

Paste printkit.dev/llms-full.txt into your coding agent. It reads the full API spec, product catalog, and integration guide.

2

Agent builds the integration

Your agent writes the code to call the API. One fetch request with an image URL, product type, and shipping address.

3

Customer checks out

They pay you at whatever price you set. You handle the payment. We handle everything after.

4

We print & ship

Printed in our US lab with archival inks on premium substrates. Packed and shipped with tracking to their door.
