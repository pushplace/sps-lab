Everything you need to know about the PrintKit partner program and how commissions work.

Commission varies by product but averages around $15 per order. Premium products like gallery frames ($34) and wood prints ($22) yield higher commissions. There are no tiers or caps — you earn the same rate from order one.
No. PrintKit sets retail pricing to ensure competitive, market-tested prices that convert. This keeps things simple — your customers always see consistent, fair pricing regardless of which partner app they use.
Payouts are issued monthly via direct deposit or PayPal. Commissions are tracked in real time on your partner dashboard. Minimum payout threshold is $50.
PrintKit handles all customer support, returns, and refunds. If an order is refunded, the commission for that order is deducted from your next payout. You never interact with customer complaints directly.
PrintKit manages the entire fulfillment pipeline. Orders are printed at professional US-based labs, quality-checked, packed, and shipped with tracking. Typical delivery is 5–7 business days. You never touch inventory.
Printify and Printful are print-on-demand platforms designed for e-commerce stores. PrintKit is an API built for apps and AI agents. You do not run a store — you send a print order with one API call. We handle checkout, payment, fulfillment, and customer support. It is a revenue share, not a wholesale markup model.
