07 — FAQ

Common
Questions

How much does it cost to join? +
Nothing. PrintKit is completely free to join. There are no subscription fees, setup costs, or minimum order requirements. You only pay our base cost when a customer places an order, and you keep the difference.
How do I get paid? +
Profits are deposited directly into your bank account twice per month, on the 1st and 15th. You can track all earnings in real time through your partner dashboard.
Who handles shipping and customer service? +
We do. Every order is printed, quality checked, packaged, and shipped by our production team. Our customer support handles returns, reprints, and any issues so you can focus on creating.
Can I set my own prices? +
Absolutely. You set the retail price for each product above our base cost. The entire markup is your profit. Want to sell a metal print for $29 or $99? Your call.
What's the print quality like? +
Museum-grade. We use archival inks and premium substrates—brushed aluminum for metal, real birch for wood, optical-grade acrylic for blocks. Every print is built to last decades, not months.
Do I need a website or large following? +
No. You get a dedicated storefront link that works anywhere—Instagram bio, email newsletter, text message, even a QR code on a business card. Some of our best sellers started with fewer than 1,000 followers.
How fast is shipping? +
Most orders ship within 2–4 business days from our US-based facility. Standard delivery is 5–7 business days domestically. Expedited options are available at checkout.
