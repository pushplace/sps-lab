// That's it. Seriously.
const order = await fetch(
  "https://api.printkit.com/v1/orders",
  {
    method: "POST",
    headers: {
      "Authorization":
        `Bearer ${KEY}`,
      "Content-Type":
        "application/json"
    },
    body: JSON.stringify({
      product: "metal_8x10",
      image_url: "...photo.jpg",
      quantity: 1,
      ship_to: {
        name: "Jane Doe",
        city: "Portland",
        state: "OR"
      }
    })
  }
);

the deal

[MARGIN]
You keep 100% of the markup. Our cost is your cost. Whatever you charge above that is yours.
[BRAND]
White-label everything. Your packaging, your brand, your customer relationship. We're invisible.
[QUALITY]
15 years of printing expertise. Lab-grade equipment. Color-calibrated output. No shortcuts.
[SCALE]
No volume minimums. 1 order or 10,000 orders. Same API, same cost, same quality.
[SPEED]
Ships in 2-4 business days. US-based production. Real tracking on every order.
[API]
Built for agents and automation. RESTful. Webhooks. Idempotent. What your code expects.
[PAYOUT]
Automatic payouts. Collect from customers, pay us wholesale. Margin never leaves your account.
[ZERO]
$0 to start. No setup fees, no monthly minimums, no commitment. Pay per order at wholesale.

// frequently asked questions

FAQ.parse()

You pay us wholesale cost per product. You charge your end customer whatever you want. The difference is your revenue. No percentage split, no commission. Your margin = retail price - wholesale cost. You keep 100% of the markup.
Zero. No minimum order volume, no monthly fees, no setup costs. Get an API key, make a request, get charged wholesale for that one order. Scale when you're ready.
You collect payment from your users directly (Stripe, whatever you use). We charge your account the wholesale cost when an order ships. Your margin stays in your account from the start -- we never touch it.
Yes. The API is designed for agent-first usage. Standard REST, JSON in/out, idempotent operations, webhook notifications. Your agent can create orders, check status, and handle shipping without any human in the loop.
We run our own production labs with 15 years of experience. Every print is color-calibrated and inspected. Image validation at the API level rejects low-res uploads before they become bad prints. If something goes wrong, we reprint at our cost.
Currently 30+ countries. US orders ship from domestic labs in 2-4 business days. International orders ship from the nearest production facility. All orders include tracking.
Completely. No PrintKit branding on packaging, labels, or products. As far as your customer knows, it came from you. Custom packing slips and branded inserts available on request.
