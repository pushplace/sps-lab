the entire integration
javascript 
// That's it. Seriously.
const order = await fetch("https://api.printkit.com/v1/orders", {
  method: "POST",
  headers: {
    "Authorization": `Bearer ${PRINTKIT_API_KEY}`,
    "Content-Type": "application/json"
  },
  body: JSON.stringify({
    product: "metal_8x10",
    image_url: "https://example.com/photo.jpg",
    quantity: 1,
    ship_to: {
      name: "Jane Doe",
      street: "123 Main St",
      city: "Portland",
      state: "OR",
      zip: "97201"
    }
  })
});

// frequently asked questions

FAQ.parse()

You pay us the wholesale cost per product. You charge your end customer whatever you want. The difference is your revenue. There's no percentage split, no commission taken. Your margin = retail price - wholesale cost. You keep 100% of the markup.
Zero. No minimum order volume, no monthly fees, no setup costs. You get an API key, you make a request, you get charged wholesale for that one order. Scale when you're ready.
You collect payment from your users directly (Stripe, whatever you use). We charge your account the wholesale cost when an order ships. Your margin stays in your account from the start -- we never touch it.
Yes. The API is designed for agent-first usage. Standard REST, JSON in/out, idempotent operations, webhook notifications. Your agent can create orders, check status, and handle shipping without any human in the loop.
We run our own production labs with 15 years of experience. Every print is color-calibrated and inspected. Image validation happens at the API level -- we'll reject low-resolution uploads before they become bad prints. If something goes wrong, we reprint at our cost.
Currently 30+ countries. US orders ship from domestic labs in 2-4 business days. International orders ship from the nearest production facility. All orders include tracking.
Completely. No PrintKit branding on packaging, shipping labels, or products. As far as your customer knows, it came from you. Custom packing slips and branded inserts are available on request.
