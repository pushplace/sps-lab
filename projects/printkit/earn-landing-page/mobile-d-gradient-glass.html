FAQ

Common questions

How much does it cost to get started?
+
Nothing. There's no signup fee, no monthly charge, and no minimum order volume. You only pay wholesale cost when a customer places an order. The margin between your retail price and wholesale cost is yours.
How do I set my prices?
+
You have complete control. Set any retail price above the wholesale cost. We suggest a 2-3x markup for competitive pricing, but you can go higher for premium positioning or lower for volume.
What about shipping and handling?
+
We handle all production, quality control, packaging, and shipping. Products ship from US facilities. Domestic orders typically arrive in 5-7 business days. You can also charge customers for shipping and keep that margin too.
Can I white-label the packaging?
+
Yes. White-labeling is included at no extra cost. Your brand goes on the box, packing slip, and return label. Customers never see PrintKit mentioned anywhere in the experience.
How long does integration take?
+
Most developers integrate the core ordering flow in a few hours to one day. Our REST API is straightforward: submit an image with product and shipping details, and we handle the rest. SDKs are available for popular platforms.
What's the print quality like?
+
Professional-grade. We use the same production partners trusted by major photo brands. Metal prints are dye-sublimated on ChromaLuxe aluminum. Wood prints use direct UV printing. Frames use archival giclée printing. Every product goes through quality checks.
How and when do I get paid?
+
You collect payment directly from your customers using your own payment processor (Stripe, etc). You pay us the wholesale cost per order. The margin stays in your account immediately. There's no waiting period or payout cycle on our end.
