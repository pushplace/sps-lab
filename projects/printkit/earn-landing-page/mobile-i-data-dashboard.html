FAQ

Common questions.

How much can I actually earn?

+
It depends on your volume and pricing. A partner sending 100 orders per month at average margins earns roughly $1,500/mo. Our top partners sending 1,000+ orders earn $15,000 or more monthly. You control the retail price, so you control the margin.

What does integration look like?

+
Our REST API and SDKs (iOS, Android, React Native, Flutter) make integration straightforward. Most partners go live within a few days. We provide sandbox testing, sample projects, and dedicated integration support.

Do customers know about PrintKit?

+
Never. Everything is 100% white-label. Packaging, shipping labels, and tracking pages all use your brand. Your customers see only your name. We're invisible infrastructure behind your experience.

What about print quality?

+
We use professional-grade equipment and materials. Metal prints on real aluminum with a dye-sublimation process. Framed prints with museum-quality archival paper. Every product is inspected before shipping. We stand behind the quality with a satisfaction guarantee.

How and when do I get paid?

+
You collect payment directly from your users through your own payment system. You pay us the wholesale cost per order. The margin is yours immediately. No waiting for payouts, no revenue share -- you keep everything above our wholesale price.

What are the shipping times?

+
Standard production is 2-4 business days. Standard shipping is 4-7 business days. We also offer expedited and priority options. Tracking numbers are provided via webhook for every order, so you can keep your customers updated in real time.

Is there a minimum order volume?

+
No minimums, no maximums, and no monthly commitments. Whether you send us 1 order or 10,000 orders per month, you get the same wholesale pricing. Start small, test the waters, and scale when you're ready. The API is always on.
