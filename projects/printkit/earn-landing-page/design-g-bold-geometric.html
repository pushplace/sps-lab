FAQ

Straight answers.

Everything you need to know before you start building.

How does pricing work?

We charge you a wholesale cost per product. You set any retail price you want. The difference is your profit. No subscriptions, no platform fees, no hidden charges. You only pay when an order ships.

What's the minimum order volume?

There is no minimum. Send one order or ten thousand. Our pricing stays the same. We're built for partners who start small and scale fast.

How fast do orders ship?

Most orders are printed and shipped within 2-4 business days. Domestic delivery is typically 3-7 days after shipping. We provide real-time tracking via webhooks so you always know the status.

Is it really white-label?

Yes. Your customer sees your brand, not ours. We ship in plain or custom-branded packaging. The return address, packing slip, and any inserts can be customized to your brand.

Can AI agents use the API directly?

Absolutely. PrintKit is agent-first. Simple JSON payloads, predictable response schemas, clear error codes. We also provide an MCP server and tool definitions for popular agent frameworks. Your AI agent can place orders autonomously.

What happens if there's a quality issue?

We reprint or refund at our cost. Every order is quality-checked before shipping. If anything isn't right, we make it right. Your reputation with your customers is our top priority.
