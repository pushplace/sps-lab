FAQ

Common questions.

Everything you need to know about the PrintKit partner program.

How do I get started? +
Sign up for a partner account, grab your API key, and make your first test order. The whole process takes about 5 minutes. Our docs walk you through every step.
What does it cost to join? +
Nothing. There are no monthly fees, no minimums, and no setup costs. You only pay the wholesale price when an order is placed. If you send zero orders, you pay zero.
How much can I charge my customers? +
Whatever you want. You set the retail price in your app. The margin between your retail price and our wholesale cost is yours to keep. We have no influence over your pricing.
How fast do orders ship? +
Most products ship within 2-4 business days from our US-based production facilities. We handle all printing, quality control, packaging, and shipping. You get tracking updates via webhooks.
How do payouts work? +
Payouts happen automatically every 14 days via ACH or wire transfer. You can track your balance, pending payouts, and payout history in real-time on your partner dashboard.
Is the API hard to integrate? +
Not at all. It's a standard REST API. Upload an image, select a product, provide a shipping address, and submit. One API call creates an order. We have SDKs for Python, Node.js, and Swift.
What happens with returns? +
We handle all customer service for print quality issues. If there's a defect, we reprint and reship at no cost to you. Your margin is never at risk for quality issues on our end.
Can I white-label the experience? +
Yes. All packaging and shipping labels can be fully white-labeled with your brand. Your customers never know PrintKit is involved. It's your product, your brand, your experience.
