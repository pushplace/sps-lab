Questions?

What if I don't have a photo for every month?
No problem. Fill as many slots as you have. 8 photos, 10 photos, whatever you've got. We'll space them evenly and it still looks great. You can always order a new one later when you have the full set.
Are the photos too small to see?
Each photo prints at 1.5" x 1.5" on premium matte magnetic material. Crisp, clear, and instantly recognizable from across the kitchen. Modern phone cameras capture more than enough detail.
Does it actually work as a ruler?
Yes! Real inch markings along the edge. You can hold it up to your kid for a fun growth check. Though we recommend a doorframe for official measurements — fridges aren't known for accuracy.
What about next year?
That's the best part. Order Year Two, Year Three, and beyond. Build a collection on the fridge that tells the whole story. We'll even send you a reminder when it's time to make the next one.
Can I put it on the wall instead of the fridge?
The magnetic backing is designed for fridges, but we include a small adhesive strip if you'd rather mount it on a wall or doorframe. Works great either way.
How long does shipping take?
Ships in 3-5 business days. Your strip arrives in a protective tube so it's perfectly flat and ready to stick on the fridge right out of the box.
