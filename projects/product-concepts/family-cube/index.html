Questions?

What if my family has fewer than 6 people?
Use any face you want. 4 family members + 2 pets works great. Or use 5 family members and make one face a group photo. You could even do the same person at different ages for a baby's first year cube.
How good is the photo quality?
Each face tile is about 18mm x 18mm. At that size, UV printing produces sharp, vivid results from any modern phone photo. We'll flag it during upload if your photo resolution is too low.
Do the faces line up perfectly when solved?
Yes. Our grid alignment editor makes sure the tiles form a coherent portrait when the side is solved. The printing is registered to the tile edges so alignment is precise. It's not pixel-perfect photography, but it's instantly recognizable and that's the whole point.
Is this a good-quality Rubik's cube?
It's a smooth-turning 56mm cube — the same mechanism used in speed cubes. It won't lock up or feel cheap. The UV print adds no noticeable thickness to the tiles.
Can I include pets?
Absolutely. We strongly recommend it. The scrambled combinations with a pet face (dog nose where dad's nose should be) are by far the funniest results. One pet minimum is our unofficial recommendation.
Is this a good gift?
It's the best gift. Arrives in a gift box. The moment someone opens it and sees their family on a Rubik's cube, the reaction is guaranteed. Then the scrambling starts and it's 30 minutes of laughing. We've seen it.
