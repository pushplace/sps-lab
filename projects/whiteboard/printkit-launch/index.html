Decisions to Make Before Launch
Decision 01
How do developers pay for prints?
Do devs pre-fund an account (Stripe balance), pay per order (card on file), or get invoiced monthly? Affects Alejo's billing work.

Decision 02
What's in the product catalog at launch?
Currently 6 products. Do we launch with all 6, or just the simplest ones (large format prints, metal prints)? More products = more edge cases.
All 6
Prints only

Decision 03
Photo handling: URL reference or upload?
Option A: dev passes a photo URL, we download it. Simple but we depend on the URL staying live. Option B: presigned upload to our S3. More reliable but more API complexity.
URL reference
Upload to S3
Support both
Decision 04
Pricing model — markup or commission?
Cost-plus: dev pays base cost, sets their own retail price, keeps the markup. Commission: we set retail, dev gets X% per sale. Cost-plus is simpler and more dev-friendly.
Cost-plus
Commission
Both options
